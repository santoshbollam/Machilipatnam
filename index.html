Kalamkari - The Art of Storytelling

Machilipatnam is renowned for its exquisite Kalamkari art, a traditional craft that involves hand-painting or block-printing textiles with natural dyes. This ancient art form tells stories through intricate designs featuring mythological characters, flora, and fauna.

Transforming into a Global Maritime Hub

Machilipatnam is poised to become one of India's premier port cities, combining its rich historical heritage with cutting-edge infrastructure. The new deep-water port will handle over 100 million tonnes of cargo annually, creating thousands of jobs and attracting global trade.
